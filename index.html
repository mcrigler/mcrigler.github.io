About Me


I am a native Texan, born on Fort Bliss in El Paso, and as an "Army brat" I have live across the US and in several location in Germany. I am a proud graduate of Eliison High School in Killeen, Texas "Go Eagles" and of the University of Texas at Austin "Hook Em Horns".

I have worked for the Travis CAD for more than 25 year. I am an Crock Pot guru and an expert at baking with liquor. My dog is named Pepsi which matches her effervescent personality (she is a big goofy ball of energy). I'd rather read a book than watch TV and thank the heavens for Kindle Reader because it has decluttered my house.
